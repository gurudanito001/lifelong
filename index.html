    The Learn Life Skills in Nigeria Bootcamp is a 2-Week in-residence program for high potential 13-17 year olds to learn world class practical training, inspire innovation, drive success in the marketplace and ultimately lead to transformation of the Nigerian economy.

    What We Do

    We teach pre-university teens (13 - 17) life skills and mentor them for life.

    We provide hands on and project based learning on collaboration, communication, critical thinking (design thinking), creativity, problem solving, technology and leadership.

    How It Works

    Enrollment

    Enroll for a 2-Week Learn Life Skills in Nigeria Bootcamp.
    We have specially designed our curriculum to reflect current and future realities.

    Training

    Our Faculty has prepared a curriculum that is fun, relevant and prepares students for life.
    Our bootcamp is very intensive and usually lasts for 2 weeks.

    Mentoring

    At the end of each boot camp, participants are attached to a relevant mentor who guides the student for a year to achieve their true worth/potential.
